The Best Animes According to Alfi

My Top 3 Animes of All-Time

Kono Oto Tomare

Favourite anime with friendship story and musical genre. I love the song that they used in here.

Dr. Stone

Science-fictional anime with no complicated problems. Enjoyable and fun anime to watch.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Action-fictional anime that are fun to watch. Especially if you want to have adrenaline sensation in every episodes.
